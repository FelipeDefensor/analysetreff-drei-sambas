{1} "To sing" and "to hit on" use the same word "cantar" in Brazilian portuguese.
{2} Favelas in Rio de Janeiro are usually on more elevated regions ("hills", colloquially), away from the more expensive real-estate that is closer to the shoreline and to sea level. For decades most of them were not pavemented, and the term "asphalt" became a slang for referring to the richer rest of the city.
{3} The original translates literally to "If you're seeing a square sun rise". The expression comes from the fact that windows in prison cells are square and small, meaning you only get to see a small square section of it.
